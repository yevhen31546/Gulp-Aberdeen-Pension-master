DO YOU NEED TO ENTER YOUR PENSION CONTRIBUTIONS ON YOUR TAX RETURN?

The answer for most colleagues is ‘No you don’t’

This is because any employee contributions are usually made by salary sacrifice, which reduces your taxable income in return for an increased employer contribution

However, there are 3 occasions where you may have to complete pension details on your tax return:

  • If you have made any ad hoc contributions from your bank account

  • If you are making employee contributions outside of salary sacrifice

  • If you have exceeded the Annual Allowance and you have insufficient unused allowance from previous years
