ABERDEEN SCOOPS PENSION SCHEME OF THE YEAR

Aberdeen has won Defined Contribution (DC) Pension Scheme of the Year and Pension Scheme of the Year at this year's Professional Pensions UK Pensions Awards.

Held in London, they are the largest UK institutional industry awards event of the year, with a number of hight-profile consultants and trustees on the judging panel.

Aberdeen won DC Pension Scheme of the Year (for firms with under 5,000 staff), beating DP World Group, RSM and Schneider Electric.

We were then delighted to win Pension Scheme of the Year - to win this coveted award we beat off stiff competition from all 17 category winners, including British Airways, Vodafone UK, BNP Paribas, HSBC, Citi UK, Lloyds Bank and Nationwide.

The key areas where our pension offering stood out against the competition -

1. Innovation: Lemonade's innovative portal helps all colleagues understand the complex and changing pension environment, including the new pension allowances.

2. Investment management: we have launched new fund profiles to match your lifestyle needs.

3. Communication: the combination of Lemonade's free one-to-one advice, educational roadshows and a robust consultative committee (the EPCC) keeps all colleagues informed.

The Employee Pension Consultation Committee (EPCC) works extensively with our pension provider, Aegon, and pension advisers, Lemonade to ensure Aberdeen continues to provide a first class pension aligned to your needs.
